4-Day Jaipur Royal Heritage Tour

Duration: 3 Nights / 4 Days
Destination: Jaipur, Rajasthan
Ideal For: Culture lovers, families, history buffs, couples

₹18,000 – ₹22,000 Includes hotel, meals, local cab, adventure activities (optional extra)

Itenerary Overview

    Day 1: Arrival in Jaipur

  • Check-in at hotel

  • Visit City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal

  • Explore local markets

    Day 2: Fort Exploration

  • Amer Fort with optional elephant ride

  • Jal Mahal stopover

  • Light & Sound show at Amer (optional)

    Day 3: Forts & Culture

  • Visit Nahargarh Fort and Jaigarh Fort

  • Albert Hall Museum

  • Cultural dinner (optional)

    Day 4: Local Shopping & Departure

  • Handicrafts, jewelry, textiles

  • Departure with royal memories

    Day 5: Naggar Castle & Art Gallery

  • Visit Naggar Castle, Nicholas Roerich Art Gallery

  • Optional river rafting in Beas (seasonal)

    Day 6: Leisure Day

  • Free for shopping, café hopping, or relaxing with nature

    Optional spa or Ayurvedic massage

    Day 7: Departure

Amenities Provided

  • Heritage-style hotel stay

  • Guided city tours (Amber Fort, City Palace, Hawa Mahal)

  • Daily breakfast + traditional Rajasthani dinner

  • Folk dance and cultural show (optional)

  • Air-conditioned private transport

  • Shopping assistance for local bazaars
