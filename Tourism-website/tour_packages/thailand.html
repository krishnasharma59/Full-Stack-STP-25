7-Day Thailand Tropical & Cultural Tour

Duration: 6 Nights / 7 Days
Destination: Bangkok, Pattaya, Phuket
Ideal For: Beach lovers, party goers, cultural explorers

Estimated Price: ₹60,000 – ₹80,000 per person (6–7 Days)

Itenerary Overview

    Day 1: Arrival in Bangkok

  • Check-in and evening Chao Phraya River Cruise

    Day 2: Bangkok City Tour

  • Grand Palace, Wat Pho, Emerald Buddha

  • Shopping at MBK or Siam Paragon

    Day 3: Travel to Pattaya

  • Alcazar Cabaret Show in the evening

    Day 4: Coral Island Tour

  • Speedboat ride, parasailing, banana boat (optional)

    Day 5: Fly to Phuket

  • Relax at Patong Beach

    Day 6: Phi Phi Islands Day Trip

  • Speedboat tour, snorkeling, Maya Bay visit

    Day 7: Departure

Amenities Provided

  • 3*/4* Hotel stays with breakfast (Bangkok, Pattaya, Phuket)

  • Airport pick-up & drop-off

  • Domestic travel between cities (flight/bus)

  • Coral Island Tour with water sports (optional)

  • Chao Phraya Dinner Cruise (Bangkok)

  • Phi Phi Island or James Bond Island tour (Phuket)

  • Sightseeing tours with guide & AC transport

  • Visa assistance (Visa on arrival available for Indians)
