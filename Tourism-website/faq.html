Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’ve got answers!

From booking to travel tips, we’ve compiled the most common queries to help you plan your trip with ease and confidence. If you can’t find what you’re looking for, feel free to contact us directly

Q1. How can I book a tour package from your website?

A: Simply visit our "Packages" page, choose your destination, and click the "Book Now" button. Fill in your details and we’ll get in touch for confirmation and payment.

Q2. What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and Google Pay. For group bookings or international trips, bank transfers are also available.

Q3. Can I customize my tour package?

A: Yes, most of our packages are customizable based on your preferences for destinations, hotel types, travel dates, and activities.

Q4. What’s included in the tour package cost?

A: Generally, packages include accommodation, local transportation, sightseeing, and meals. Exclusions (like entry fees or flight tickets) are mentioned clearly in each package.

Q5. Will I get a refund if I cancel my trip?

A: Refund policies vary by package and timing. Cancellations made 7 days before the trip usually receive a partial refund. Details are shared during booking.

Q6. Do you provide visa assistance for international trips?

A: Yes, we help with basic visa guidance and document support. However, the final visa decision depends on the embassy/consulate.

Q7. Is travel insurance included?

A: Travel insurance is optional and can be added at an additional cost. We highly recommend it for international or adventure travel.

Q8. Can I get a guide during my trip?

A: Yes, we offer local guides at most destinations. You can request a guide while booking or even during the tour (subject to availability).

Q9. How far in advance should I book my tour?

A: We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for domestic trips and 1–2 months for international tours, especially during peak seasons, to ensure availability and best prices.

Q10. Do you offer discounts for group or family bookings?

A: Yes! We provide special discounts for group, family, and student bookings. Contact us with your group size and travel dates to get the best deal.
