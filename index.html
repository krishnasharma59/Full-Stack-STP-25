Indian History

Indian history encompasses a vast and rich narrative, spanning thousands of years and marked by diverse civilizations, empires, and cultural influences. Key periods include the Indus Valley Civilization, Vedic period, the rise of empires like the Maurya and Gupta, the medieval period with Islamic conquests, and the early modern era with the Mughal Empire. Finally, the British Raj and the subsequent independence movement led to the formation of modern India, Pakistan, and Bangladesh.
